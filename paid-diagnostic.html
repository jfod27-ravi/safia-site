Solution Report

You know what's wrong.
Now find out why — and what to do.

The Problem Finder Report named the constraint. The Solution Report is the second half: root cause, fix direction, and the enabling conditions your team needs in place before the fix can hold.

What the Solution Report contains
  • 01
    Root Cause Analysis
    Why the primary constraint exists at a mechanical level — not just what it produces. Includes a distortion scan of your intake responses to separate what you described from what is actually driving the problem.
  • 02
    Fix Direction & Implementation Sequence
    A step-by-step direction calibrated to your team's current tool adoption level and operational capacity. Not a generic prescription — built around your specific constraints.
  • 03
    Two Enabling Conditions
    The two structural prerequisites that must be in place before the primary fix takes hold. Most implementations fail because these are skipped.
  • 04
    Common Misdiagnoses — Ruled Out
    The three most likely wrong diagnoses for your situation — named and eliminated. So you don't spend money fixing the wrong thing.
  • 05
    Next Steps
    A prioritized action sequence. The first move, the second move, and what to stop doing immediately.
